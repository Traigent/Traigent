APPROACH:

2. Consider adding challenging questions like:
   - The Alderman riddle (logic puzzle)
   - Multi-step reasoning problems
   - Questions where verbosity matters (max_tokens constraint)
   - Questions where creativity vs precision matters (temperature)

3. Curate a 20-question subset specifically designed to:
   - Show model capability differences (gpt-3.5 vs gpt-4o)
   - Show temperature effects (creative vs factual)
   - Show token limit effects (verbose vs concise answers)

4. Expected outcome: A dataset where different configs score differently,
   making optimization meaningful rather than random selection.
